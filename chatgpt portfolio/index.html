About Oyalami

Hey there! I’m Oyalami Oluwadarasimi, a 15-year-old from Nigeria with a heart for creativity, tech, and personal growth.

As a teen graphic designer, I pour my soul into crafting vibrant, eye-catching flyers with Canva — each one a bold expression of my vision. But I’m more than just designs: I’m diving headfirst into Python to unlock the power of code, and I’m even nerding out over chemistry because learning fuels my fire!

My journey is all about chasing big dreams through small, steady steps, and I’m sharing every moment in public to inspire others. From sketching stunning visuals to coding my first app, I’m proof that a teen with a laptop can create something extraordinary.

Ready to dive into my world of bold designs and tech adventures? Explore my portfolio and join me! #DarasimiDesigns
